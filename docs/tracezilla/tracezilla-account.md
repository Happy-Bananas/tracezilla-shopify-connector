## Add a Location

Create your first location by clicking the **Partner** icon in the top
navigation bar, then click **Add Location**.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/tracezilla/add-location.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Add location"
    width="958%">
</p>

Complete the **Add Location** form and click **Save**.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/tracezilla/add-location-form.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Add Location form"
    width="958%">
</p>

------------------------------------------------------------------------
