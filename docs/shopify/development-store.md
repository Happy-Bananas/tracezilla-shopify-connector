# Create a free Shopify Development Store

After successfully signing up for a Shopify partner account you will end up on the **Get started** page.

From the sidebar to the right, click on the **Stores** link

<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/partner-dashboard-sidebar.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Partner dashboard sidebar"
    width="224">
</p>

---

Click **Dev** from the Create store dialog

<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/create-dev-store.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Create store dev"
    width="639">
</p>

---

Give your test plan a name, select a plan, they are all free for developers, make sure to check the **Generate test data for store** checkbox

<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/select-plan.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Select plan"
    width="634">
</p>

---

{: .warning }
> ⚠️ You have now been taken to the store you just created. Notice that you are no longer in the Shopify Partner Dashboard.
