The first step is to create a Shopify Partner account.

A Partner account gives you access to Shopify's developer tools,
including development stores and custom applications. Everything in this
guide is built on top of a Partner account.

## Create an Account

Visit the Shopify Partner Dashboard:

[Shopify Partner Dashboard](https://partners.shopify.com/){: .btn
.btn-green }

Click **Sign up** in the upper-right corner and follow the registration
process.

After signing up, Shopify will ask how you would like to proceed. Select
**Create and set up a new Partner organization**. If you already have a
Partner organization, simply select it instead.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/select-partner-profile.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Select Partner organization"
    width="1032">
</p>

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Select **Build new stores for clients** and click **Next**.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/build-new-store-for-clients.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Build new stores for clients"
    width="1064%">
</p>

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Select your region and click **Next**.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/select-region.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Select region"
    width="958%">
</p>

Complete your business information and verify your account.

Once your Partner account has been approved, you're ready to continue
with the tutorial.

{: .note } \> 💡 I recommend bookmarking the Shopify Partner Dashboard
so you can quickly return to it later.
