# Create a Free Shopify Development Store

After creating your Shopify Partner account, you will be taken to the
**Get started** page.

From the navigation menu on the left, click **Stores**.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/partner-dashboard-sidebar.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Partner Dashboard sidebar"
    width="224">
</p>

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Click **Dev** in the **Create store** dialog.

<p align="left">
<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/create-dev-store.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Create development store"
    width="639">
</p>

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Enter a name for your development store and choose a plan. All available
plans are free for Shopify Partners.

For this tutorial, make sure to enable **Generate test data for store**
before creating the store.

<p align="left">
`<img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/select-plan.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Select development store plan"
    width="634">
</p>

------------------------------------------------------------------------

> 💡 After the store has been created, you will
automatically be redirected to the new development store. <br/> Notice
that you are no longer in the Shopify Partner Dashboard. You can return
to the Partner Dashboard at any time using the Shopify Partners link or
by visiting the Partner Dashboard directly.
