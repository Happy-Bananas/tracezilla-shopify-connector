The first step is to create a Shopify Partner account.

A Partner account gives you access to Shopify's developer tools, including development stores and custom applications. Everything in this guide is built on top of a Partner account.

## Create an Account

Visit the Shopify Partner dashboard:

[Shopify Partner Dashboard](https://partners.shopify.com/){: .btn .btn-green }

Click **Sign up** in the top-right corner and follow the registration process.

After signing up, you will be asked how you would like to proceed. Click **Create and set up a new Partner organization**. If you already have a Partner organization, you can select that option instead.


<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/select-partner-profile.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Select partner profile"
    width="1032">
</p>

---

Select **Build new stores for clients** and click the **Next** button.

<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/build-new-store-for-clients.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Build new store for partner"
    width="1064%">
</p>

---

Select region and press the **Next** button.

<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/select-region.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Select region"
    width="958%">
</p>


After you have filled in your business information and successfully confirmed your account, you can enjoy a nice cup of coffee, smoke a cigar, or do whatever makes you happy. ☕
