# Create custom app

[Go to the Shopify development dashboard](https://dev.shopify.com/dashboard){: .btn .btn-primary }

From the navigation sidebar to the left select **Apps**


Click on the link **Create app** in the bottom right corner
<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/create-app.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Create app"
    width="1374">
</p>


If you already have an app. click on the create app botton at the top right.
<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/create-app-button.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Create app"
    width="194">
</p>

## Get credentials

When the app is created and selected you can fetch the credentials from the app settings page by selecting settings from the sidebar

<p align="left">
  <img
    src="{{ '/assets/images/shopify/click-on-settings.png' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Click on settings"
    width="260">
</p>

---

This where you will fetch the **Client ID** and the **Secret** you need to integrate your catalog with the Tracezilla API. Take a copy and store it in a safe place.


{: .warning }
> ⚠️ Your app will not authorize before you have assigned it to a store.
>
